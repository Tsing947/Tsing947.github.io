Research Interest

I work in the field of Geographical and Oceanographic Science. My research focuses on blue food system transformation, especially the role of capture fisheries and aquaculture in sustainable and resilient food systems.
More specifically, my research interests include:
  1. Integrating blue foods into broader food system frameworks to assess their contributions to sustainable food supply, ecosystem protection, and dietary transitions.
  2. Coupling capture fisheries, aquaculture, and related value chains to develop integrated models for analyzing trade-offs and synergies within blue food systems.
  3. Developing AI-assisted approaches for blue food data production and analysis, including evidence extraction, database construction, and quantitative synthesis of fisheries, aquaculture, and blue food system information.
